Arbor Lodge Tree Team

The Arbor Lodge Tree Team works to plant, preserve, and promote trees in our neighborhood and beyond. Mainly, though, we are just people who like trees.

We like that they improve our air quality and health and well-being. We like their visual appeal and cooling shade; the erosion control and stormwater benefits they provide. We like that they provide food and shelter for wildlife while enhancing our own homes’ values.

We give away fruit trees and berry bushes, and we build community. We help our Earth disperse her gifts of trees and the fruit each bears for our common good. We are “tree geeks” on a mission to strengthen the relationships and resilience of our neighborhoods.
