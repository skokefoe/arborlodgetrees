North Portland Tree Bike Rides

The Arbor Lodge Tree Team has created these family-friendly bike routes to take in notable trees in North and Northeast Portland.

Cully Neighborhood Tree Ride

For Bike Summer 2024, the Arbor Lodge Tree Team led a tour of beautiful trees in Northeast Portland's Cully Neighborhood. Start at Fernhill Park and follow along this route that ends at the arboretum at Rigler Elementary School. Click here to view the map fullscreen.

North Portland Heritage Tree Ride

The Arbor Lodge Tree Team created this bike route featuring Heritage Trees in North Portland as part of the 2023 Pedalpalooza celebration. You can recreate this route and learn about beautiful heritage trees and the stories behind them by using an app like Ride with GPS or Google Maps or print out a map to follow along.


North Portland Heritage Tree Ride

In 2022, the Arbor Lodge Tree Team created this loop ride between Columbia Park and Pier Park.
