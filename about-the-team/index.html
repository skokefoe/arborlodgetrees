About the Team

The Arbor Lodge Tree Team meets on the second Wednesday of each month to work on issues surrounding trees in our North Portland neighborhood.

We'd like to grow more trees, educate the public about the importance of trees for our health and environment, and keep the trees we have as healthy as possible.
